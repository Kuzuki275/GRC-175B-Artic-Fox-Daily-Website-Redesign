Steps to Assist a Fox in Need

Step 1: Assess the Situation

Observe the animal from a safe distance. Determine if it is truly in distress or danger.

Step 2: Contact Local Wildlife Authorities

Reach out to your local wildlife rehabilitation center or animal control for immediate assistance.

Step 3: Do Not Approach

Wild animals can be unpredictable and may carry diseases or may become aggressive. Avoid handling them directly.

Step 4: Report to Us

Submit a report using our form below. Provide as many details as possible about the animal and its condition.
